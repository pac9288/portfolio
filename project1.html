Kettle is a team of makers, doers, and passionate problem-solvers with a simple mission: use design and technology to make the world feel more human.

So how does a brand that seeks to make the world feel more human translate visually? With a combination of organic elements that center around illustrations and blur a clean yet simplistic aesthetic. A fully responsive site that combines illustration, a humanistic typeface, playful animations, and bright colors, was created.

Additional touchpoints included business cards and a room booking app to unite the SF and NYC offices, the large, medium, and small conference rooms are named after iconic San Francisco places. It's a daily reminder you have friends on the other coast. We've received some great pats on the back from HOW Magazine, Typewolf, Site Inspire, and AWWWARDS.
