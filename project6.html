bikingwest.com, a personal post-graduation journey from Buffalo NY to Boise ID

After graduating from RIT in 2105, I set out with my best friend from hight school on a cross country bike trip, it was partly a personal journey but also a ride for a loved one that has been diagnosed wtih parkinsons deseas. bikingwest.com was a site that I designed and developed to track our trip and get the word out.

The site was the main hub for people (and parents without an instagram account) to follow us and spread the word. The stie allowed a visitor to post, tweet, or donate via Indigogo.The site populated in real-time from Instagram with the hashtag #bikingwest. We received hundreds of likes and comments.
